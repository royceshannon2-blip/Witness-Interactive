# Historical Accuracy Skill

Every number, date, name, and casualty figure in Witness Interactive is locked to verified historical record. Never invent or estimate these — check references first.

## Process
1. Identify every specific claim in the scene (dates, names, numbers, places, institutions)
2. Cross-reference against the relevant facts file before writing
3. If a fact is not in the reference files, do not include it — flag it for research
4. Never "round" numbers or shift dates for narrative convenience

## Hard Rules
- Never change locked numbers (500,000–800,000 Tutsi killed, 1,177 USS Arizona crew, etc.)
- Never depict Rusesabagina's 2021 conviction as related to 1994 — it is a completely separate conflict
- RPF crimes in eastern Congo (1996–1997) — do NOT add to scene content without explicit instruction
- Gacaca ran 2005–2012, not earlier or later
- UNAMIR was cut to 270 troops after the 10 Belgians were killed, not before
- The genocide fax was January 1994, sent and ignored months before the genocide began
