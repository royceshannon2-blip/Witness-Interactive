## Key Dates
- April 6, 1994: Habyarimana's plane shot down
- April 7, 1994: Genocide begins, 10 Belgian peacekeepers killed, PM Uwilingiyimana killed
- April 7+: Roadblocks, identity card checks, systematic killings spread from Kigali
- April 19, 1994: UN votes to cut UNAMIR to 270
- July 4, 1994: RPF captures Kigali — genocide ends
- January 1994: Dallaire sends genocide fax to New York — ignored
- November 1994: ICTR established
- 2003: Dallaire publishes *Shake Hands with the Devil*
- 2004: Canadian Senate Special Committee on Rwanda
- 2005: Gacaca courts begin / Responsibility to Protect adopted at UN
- 2012: Gacaca formally ends
- 2021: Rusesabagina convicted of terrorism in Belgium (separate conflict — NOT 1994)

## RTLM
- Founded 1993 by Akazu allies
- Youth-friendly style (music, talk shows) masking Hutu Power propaganda
- Called Tutsi "inyenzi" (cockroaches), "inzoka" (snakes)
- "Cut down the tall trees" = kill the Tutsi
- During genocide: read names, license plates, hiding places in real time
- ICTR convicted key leaders — established media can constitute a tool of genocide

## Interahamwe
- "Those who attack together" — organized Hutu Power militia, not spontaneous mobs
- Youth wing of ruling MRND party
- Trained and armed by the Akazu
- Operated at cell level using Rwanda's detailed local administrative hierarchy
- Roadblock operations: checked identity cards against lists; whistles signaled movements at 3am

## Gacaca
- "Justice on the grass" — community dispute resolution adapted for genocide
- Lay judges elected from the community (not legal professionals)
- Offered reduced sentences for full public confessions
- Supported: surfaced local information, located remains, enabled truth-telling
- Criticized: due-process shortcomings, intimidation of witnesses, RPF crimes excluded
- For gameplay: confession is both genuine accountability AND strategic self-interest. Do not resolve this.

## Hotel des Mille Collines
- ~1,200 people sheltered there
- Rusesabagina used contacts, cash, alcohol to negotiate with attackers
- UN peacekeepers and European owners also played roles
- Conditions: extremely crowded, food and water shortages
- Some survivors accuse Rusesabagina of charging for rooms, water, phone calls
- 2021 conviction: terrorism charges in Belgium, related to FLN rebel group — completely separate from 1994. Always clarify this when referencing it.

## Handle With Care
- RPF ended the genocide in Rwanda April–July 1994 — accurate and should be depicted as such
- RPF also committed serious abuses in eastern Congo 1996–1997 (UN mapping report 2010) — these are excluded from gacaca, highly sensitive, do NOT add to existing scene content without explicit instruction
- "No Hutu, No Tutsi — only Rwandan" is official post-genocide policy — characters can reference it while also privately remembering 1994 in ethnic terms. This tension is real and should not be erased.
